Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 May 2026

Who we are

Arrow Word Puzzles is a free online puzzle platform where users can solve arrow word puzzles. Our website is located at https://arrow-word.github.io/ArrowWord/.

What information we collect

When you sign in using Google or Facebook, we collect only the following information from your account:

  • Your name (as it appears on your Google or Facebook profile)
  • Your email address
  • Your profile picture URL

We also store your puzzle activity, including which puzzles you have started, completed, and how many times you have used the "Check answers" feature.

We do not collect passwords, payment information, phone numbers, or any other personal data.

How we use your information

We use your information solely to:

  • Identify you when you sign in
  • Save and restore your puzzle progress across devices
  • Display your name in the puzzle interface

We do not sell, share, or disclose your information to any third parties. We do not use your information for advertising or marketing purposes.

How we store your information

Your account information and puzzle progress are stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Sign out and stop using the service at any time

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please use the data deletion link below or contact us directly.

Data deletion

To delete all your data from Arrow Word Puzzles, visit our data deletion page or contact us. All your progress data and account information will be permanently removed within 30 days.

Cookies

We use only essential session cookies required for sign-in to function. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.
