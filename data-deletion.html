Request Data Deletion

You can request the deletion of all personal data associated with your Arrow Word Puzzles account. This includes your name, email address, profile picture, and all puzzle progress.

How to delete your data:
  1. Send an email to vernonleo@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request
  3. Include the email address associated with your account
  4. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Alternatively, simply stop using the service. We do not share your data with third parties and it will not be used for any other purpose.
