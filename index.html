How it works
Solving an arrow word puzzle is simple
1

Pick a puzzle

Browse the directory and choose any puzzle you like. New ones are added regularly.

2

Follow the arrows

Each clue cell has an arrow showing which direction its answer runs. Click a clue to highlight its word.

3

Fill in the letters

Type letters into the green cells. Use Check answers to see how you're doing, or Reveal when you're stuck.

4

Celebrate!

Complete the whole puzzle correctly and get a little celebration. You deserve it.
